INT. ROOFTOP - NIGHT

Rain pours down. ROY BATTY, pale and dying, sits in the downpour. DECKARD watches, battered and exhausted.

ROY
I've seen things you people wouldn't believe.
(beat)
Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Orion.
I watched C-beams glitter in the dark near the Tannhäuser Gate.
(quietly)
All those moments will be lost in time, like tears in rain.
(long pause)
Time to die.

Roy releases a white dove. His head drops. The dove flies away into the rain.
