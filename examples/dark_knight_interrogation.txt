INT. INTERROGATION ROOM - NIGHT

The JOKER sits calmly. BATMAN looms over him.

JOKER
(laughing)
You have nothing. Nothing to threaten me with.

Nothing to do with all your strength!

BATMAN
(quietly)
Don't talk like one of them. You're not!

JOKER
No, you're right. I'm not.

(beat)

To them, you're just a freak...

(pause)

Like me.

They need you right now, but when they don't...

(pause)

They'll cast you out.

Like a leper.

BATMAN
(angry)
Their morals, their code... it's a bad joke.

Dropped at the first sign of trouble!

JOKER
(softly)
You'll see. I'll show you.

When the chips are down, these civilized people...

(beat)

They'll eat each other.
