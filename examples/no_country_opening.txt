SHERIFF BELL (V.O.)
I was sheriff of this county when I was twenty-five years old.

Hard to believe.

My grandfather was a sheriff too.

(pause)

My father...

(beat)

He never was.

I always wondered if he'd taken this job, would he have felt the same way I do.

(slowly)

I doubt it.

But I don't know.

He may have.

You can't help but compare yourself against the old timers.

Can't help but wonder how they'd have operated in these times.
